## Privacy Policy

**GreenAura: Plant Care** respects user privacy.

- This app does **not collect, store, or share** any personal or non-personal user data.
- The app works completely **offline** and does **not use any backend or server**.
- No user accounts, logins, or authentication are required.
- No third-party services, analytics, ads, or tracking tools are used.

Since no data is collected, there is no data usage, storage, or sharing involved.

If you have any questions regarding privacy, feel free to contact the developer.
